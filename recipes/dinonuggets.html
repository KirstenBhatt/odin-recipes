Dinonuggets and French Fries

Dinonuggets and French Fries Secret Recipe

This recipe is near and dear to my three old son. Anytime I ask him what he wants for dinner, this is what he says. Sometimes even for breakfast this is what he wants. Simple and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Frozen dinonuggets from the supermarket
  • Frozen fries, crinkle cut or curly is prefered
  • Ketchup
  • spray cooking oil, for ease and convience

Instructions

  1. Go to the grocery store.
  2. Go STRAIGHT to the frozen food aisle that is clearly labeled, meals and main dishes.
  3. Get the Frozen Dinonuggets
  4. Go to the frozen aisle that is clearly labeled for potatoes.
  5. Grab a bag of crinkle cut fries, the cheapest and biggest bag will do.
  6. Head over to your condiment aisle and grab the ketchup
  7. Check out, pay, drive home
  8. Follow the instructions the bags and DO NOT throw the bags away until the dishes are fully cooked and out of the oven.
  9. Serve the dinonuggets luke warm to avoid tantrums.

This meal is toddler approved, proceed with caution.
